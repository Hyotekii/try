What is RUNNING?

Running is a form of exercise where you move faster than walking, using your whole body to improve fitness, endurance, and overall health. It engages major muscles like the legs and core while strengthening the heart and lungs. Whether done as a steady jog, a fast sprint, or a long -distance run, it boosts energy, burns calories, and helps reduce stress. Running is a simple yet powerful way to stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Running comes in many forms, each serving a different purpose. Jogging is a slow, steady pace ideal for beginners and general fitness, while Long-distance running builds stamina for races like 5Ks or marathons. Sprinting focuses on short bursts of speed to develop power, and Interval Running alternates fast and slow periods to improve endurance and burn calories efficiently. Fartlek Running mixes speeds and distances for a flexible, fun workout, while Tempo Running maintains a sustained, challenging pace to increase speed over time. Trail and Hill Running add variety by using natural terrains, strengthening muscles, improving balance, and boosting overall cardiovascular fitness. Together, these types make running adaptable for any fitness level and goal.
